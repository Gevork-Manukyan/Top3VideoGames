Introduction

   People have always played games throughout history. From the classics like chess and card games, to the more modern board games and video games, people have always loved putting their skills to the test. And with the invention of video games, came a new boom for gaming as it openned the door for endless possibilities. With the limitations of the physical world removed, more and more people flocked to cyber space. Today gaming has become one of the most common hobbies among the new generation, and over the years people have carved out subcommunities or genres within the gaming world based on the style of games they enjoy. These genres range from first person shooters, to singleplayer story games, to MMO RPGs, and tons more. There is one specific genre of games, however, that I really enjoy playing playing are roguelikes.
